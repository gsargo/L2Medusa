Special items have been implemented in order to provide a fair and entertaining environment.
Adamantine Jewelry Box(A-grade): By opening this box you can acquire one Top A Grade jewel. This jewel is equippable in Olympiad.
Blessed Tateossian Jewels(S-grade): Improved version of Tateossian Jewels.
Pandora's Silver Box: By opening this box you acquire rewards such as: Lifestones, Book of Giant, Blessed Scroll: Enchant Weapon/Armor.
Pandora's Golden Box: By opening this box you acquire rewards such as: Lifestones, Book of Giant, Blessed Scroll: Enchant Weapon/Armor.
Bag of Wonders: By opening this bag you can acquire an one time use of an Active Lv10 Skill.
Elemental Stones: There are 3 versions (Fire, Water, Earth) and they bestow special abilities to a weapon.
Clickable Maps: By clicking this type of map, a secret Clan Boss location is revealed.
Tartarus Key: Grant access at the hidden layers of Tartarus.
Asphodel Key: Grant access at the hidden layers of Asphodel Meadows.
Color Palette: By clicking this item, you acquire a special name or title color.
Kykeon: This potion greatly restores CP.
Ambrosia: This potion greatly restores HP.
Nectar: This potion greatly restores MP.
